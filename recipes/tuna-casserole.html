Tuna Casserole

Photo: Mama Loves Food

Description

Tuna casserole is a staple among staples. Its a recipe that's quick, filling, and cheap. It's easily a comfort food favorite!

As bland as its ingredients can be, this recipe is very versatile. It can be doctor-ed up with different seasonings, can have vegetables added or replaced. This is just one variation, feel free to experiement!

Ingredients

  • 2 (5 oz) cans of tuna, drained
  • 16 oz wide egg noodles
  • 10.5 oz can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 packet onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 15 oz can peas
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 2 cups fried onions

Steps

  1. Cook noodles per package instructions in salted water. Drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  3. Combine tuna, cream of mushroom soup, onion soup mix, mayonnaise, milk, peas, and worcestershire sauce in large mixing bowl.
  4. Add cooked noodles to tuna mixture, stir until coated.
  5. Pour tuna and noodle mixture into a 13 x 9 baking dish, spread evenly.
  6. Top mixture with cheese and fried onions.
  7. Bake for 30 mins or until cheese is bubbly.
